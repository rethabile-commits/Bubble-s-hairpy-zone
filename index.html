About Us

Your Hair, Nail & Lash Care Center

Welcome to BUBBLE'S HAIRPY ZONE, where passion meets creativity. Our story began 8 years ago, when our founder Nokuthula Salanyane had a vision to create a salon that would provide exceptional hair services in a warm and welcoming environment. Today we're proud to be one of the leading salons in Etwatwa, with a team of talented stylists dedicated to making you look and feel your best.

Our expert nail technicians who are passionate about creating a stunning nail designs that bring out the best in you. With years of experirience and ongoing training, our team is dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest nail trends and techniques.

We're also passionate about helping you achieve the lashes of your dreams. Our mission is to provide lash services that exceed our client's expectations.We believe that everyone deserves to have beautiful, healthy-looking lashes, and we're committed to helping you achieve that.
